FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do you estimate carbon?

We combine energy metrics per cloud region + grid carbon intensity to estimate emissions per GB transferred and CPU/GPU‑s.

Can I bring my own bucket or data lake?

Yes. We support BYO‑Storage (S3, GCS, ADLS) with async indexing and customer‑managed KMS keys.

Are there public samples?

Yes, most collections include tiles and ready‑to‑run notebooks for you to try.

How does the Gold/Silver/Bronze rank work?

We rank by metadata completeness, update reliability, coverage, and validated quality.
